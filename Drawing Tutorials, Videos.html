A Curiosity Comic, Build Your Own Rover 3D Model

The Loving Family of Curiosity!

View this HyperLapse tutorial to learn the structure of NASA's Curiosity Rover along with its components through this short fun comic!

So, what's the purpose?

The idea behind this tutorial is to teach what the overall body construction and engineering of the Curiosity rover involves through an effective and fun artistic technique.

On to the comic!

This section is here so that can you can read the comic made in the above video. Its a short, heart-touching concept for sure!

Try drawing along!

Don't aim for perfection in your artistic masterpiece. The purpose is to learn and have fun!

Build a Curiosity 3D model!

This video involves a tutorial of building a mock 3D model of the Curiosity rover in a simple way with the help of materials that are easily accessible and available.

How does this help?

The idea behind this video is to link everyday simple arts to something as complex as a car-sized rover on Mars! Building along will provide a clear idea of the rover design in a fun-filled way!
