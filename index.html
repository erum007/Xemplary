About The Site

Mars has always marvelled researchers due to the possibility of its inhabition. NASA in particular, has conducted many missions that aim to gather information of the Martian surface. Mars Science Laboratory (MSL), which led to the creation of the Curiosity rover, is one of them, and that is the mission we have targetted, so that it may be known to all!

Our site contains interactive content and engrossing activities which are not only enjoyable, but also easily understandable as our site and its content consists of:

  • Simple Language
  • Multiple Language Support
  • Creative and straight-forward tactics

Our team is a group of passionate individuals consisting of two high school students and two of their devoted teachers. Each member contributed in different ways throughout the challenge.
